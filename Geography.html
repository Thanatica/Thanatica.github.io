I. Introduction

This section is NOT a comprehensive view of the geography of Russia as there are millions of kilometers of land. Instead, to narrow the scope of this section, I will be providing what is necessary in order to gather the geographic context between the people and the land, because the climate zones, especially the animals within, are embedded deeply into many cultures, which reflects in their literature.

  • Oblast (область) : administrative sub-divisions in Post-Soviet states, their word for states or provinces.
  • Autonomous Okrug (автономный округ) : autonomous regions Russia. There are four in total, only one of which is truly autonomous while the others are under the jurisdiction of an oblast.
  • Kray / Krai (край) : territory.
  • Republics : Nation-states for ethnic minorities yet under control of the central government. They have their own constitution, official languages, and national anthems.1
